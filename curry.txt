Ingredients:
 - 3 tablespoon of oil
 - 1 teaspoon cumin
 - 1 teaspoon mustard seed
 - 1 teaspoon minapappu
 - 1 teaspoon karam
 - 1 endumirapakai
 - 1 cup chopped onions
 - 1 cup chopped vegitables
 
Directions:
 1. add oil to a pan and heat it. add cumin, mustard, minapappu
 
 2. add onions and cook then till it is transperent. Add vegitables and cook them
 
 3. Add karam and switch off the stove
